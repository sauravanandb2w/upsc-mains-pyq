# UPSC Mains PYQs (Previous Year Questions) – GS Paper 1 \[Subject-Wise\]

Preparing for the **UPSC Civil Services Mains Exam**? One of the most effective strategies is analyzing **Previous Year Questions (PYQs)**. This page provides a **subject-wise compilation of GS Paper 1 PYQs**, helping aspirants understand exam trends and refine their preparation.

## Why Should You Practice UPSC Mains GS Paper 1 PYQs?

* **Exam-Oriented Preparation:** UPSC often repeats themes, making PYQs invaluable.
* **Understand Question Trends:** Analyze how questions are framed and their evolution over the years.
* **Master Answer-Writing Skills:** PYQs help in structuring answers and improving content depth.
* **Comprehensive Coverage:** Questions span across key subjects like _History, Indian Society, and Geography_.
