# UPSC Mains PYQs (Previous Year Questions) – GS Paper 4 \[Subject-Wise\]

Preparing for the **UPSC Civil Services Mains Exam**? One of the most effective strategies is analyzing **Previous Year Questions (PYQs)**. This page provides a **subject-wise compilation of GS Paper 4 PYQs**, helping aspirants understand exam trends and refine their preparation.

## Why Should You Practice UPSC Mains GS Paper 4 PYQs?

* **Exam-Oriented Preparation:** UPSC often repeats themes, making PYQs invaluable.
* **Understand Question Trends:** Analyze how questions are framed and their evolution over the years.
* **Master Ethical Decision-Making:** PYQs help in developing a structured approach to ethical dilemmas and case studies.
* **Comprehensive Coverage:** Questions span across key areas like _Ethics, Integrity, Aptitude, Emotional Intelligence, Public Administration, and Governance_.

Ethics and Human Interface

2025

* “It is not enough to talk about peace, one must believe in it; and it is not enough to believe in it, one must act upon it.” In the present context, the major weapon industries of the developed nations are adversely influencing continuation of number of wars for their own self-interest, all around the world. What are the ethical considerations of the powerful nations in today’s international arena to stop continuation of ongoing conflicts?
