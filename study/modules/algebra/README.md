# Algebra

**Math Paper II · Section A**

Drop scanned notebook pages into the subfolders below, then list filenames in `manifest.json` → `git add` → `git push`. They appear in the app under **Modules**.

| Folder | What to scan |
|--------|----------------|
| `standard-results/` | Formulas & standard results to memorize |
| `derivations/` | Key derivations (step-by-step from your notes) |
| `tricks/` | Shortcuts, patterns, exam tricks |
| `important-questions/` | Tough PYQs / practice problems (photo or typed `README.md`) |

## Quick text notes (synced when signed in)

Use the text areas in the app for searchable summaries. Scans are for handwriting you cannot retype quickly.

## PYQs

Add questions to `data/math-paper-2.json` with `"moduleId": "algebra"` and `"section": "A"`.
